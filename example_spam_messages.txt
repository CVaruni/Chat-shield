# Money/Prize Related Spam
1. "CONGRATULATIONS! You've won FREE MONEY! Claim your $10,000 prize now!!!"
   - Triggers: free money, claim now, $amount, multiple exclamations
   
2. "Make money fast! Earn $5,000/week from home. 100% guaranteed success!"
   - Triggers: make money, work from home, $amount, exclamations

# Investment/Financial Spam
3. "EXCLUSIVE investment opportunity! Double your money in 30 days. Limited time offer!"
   - Triggers: investment opportunity, double your, limited offer, exclamations

4. "Instant credit card approved! No credit check needed. Contact now: loans@example.com"
   - Triggers: credit card approved, email present, exclamation

# Medical/Health Spam
5. "Buy cheap viagra pills online! Special discount offer - 90% off. Order at: pharmacy@spam.com or call 555-123-4567"
   - Triggers: buy pills, special offer, email, phone number

# Gambling/Casino Spam
6. "ONLINE CASINO BONUSES!!! Get rich quick playing slots. Visit www.fake-casino.com NOW!"
   - Triggers: casino, get rich, suspicious URL, excessive caps

# Work/Income Spam
7. "WORK FROM HOME!!! Make $10k/month. No experience needed! Contact: jobs@scam.com"
   - Triggers: work from home, $amount, email, multiple exclamations

# Multiple Signal Spam
8. "LIMITED TIME OFFER!!! Get FREE CASH now! Email: money@scam.com or call 555-987-6543 Visit: www.get-rich-quick.com"
   - Triggers: limited offer, free cash, email, phone, suspicious URL, multiple exclamations

Each message above demonstrates different combinations of spam signals that our detector looks for:
- Suspicious phrases and patterns
- Money amounts ($XXX, $X,XXX/month)
- Contact information (email + phone)
- Formatting (ALL CAPS, multiple !!!)
- Suspicious URLs
